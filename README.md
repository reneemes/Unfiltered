# **Unfiltered**
We chose the name Unfiltered to reflect our focus on supporting a younger generation as they navigate mental health and learn to understand the emotional world around them.

## **Description**
This app was inspired by the E 

    -   H - Health
    -   O - Oppression
    -   P - Pollution/Planet Wellness
    -   E - Education
- - -
 Our team wanted to emphasize mental health awareness, particularly among youth, as they face growing emotional challenges, societal pressures, and a lack of emotional education. Unfiltered aims to create a safe, supportive space where users can learn, reflect, and grow emotionally.
